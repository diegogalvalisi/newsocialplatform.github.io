Time to choose a healthier lifestyle!

Every year, 700,000 people in the EU lose their lives because of tobacco consumption. It is the leading cause of preventable cancer in Europe.

Here’s what we’re doing in the EU to promote healthier lifestyles:
Creating smoke-free environments
Regulating packaging, labelling & ingredients of tobacco products
Restricting advertising
Investing €96 m in research & addiction prevention

Our goal: a tobacco-free generation by 2040!

EU is getting closer to hitting 2030 climate and energy targets!

Thanks to stronger action across Member States, by 2030 we are now on track to:

Cut net greenhouse gas emissions by 55%
Reach at least 42.5% renewable energy

We’re committed to continuing the clean energy transition while prioritising industrial competitiveness and social fairness.

We will keep working together with the Member States to close the remaining gaps.
